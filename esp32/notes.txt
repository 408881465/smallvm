The boot and partition files are from Arduino esp32 development system.

The ed1_xxx files were dumped from a new(?) Citilab ED1 board.
The ED1 files are different from the Arduino ones but appear to be compatible with the MicroBlocks VM.

Address (hex)		Arduino ESP32			ED1
0E00			boot_app0.bin				ed1_E00.bin
1000			bootloader_dio_80m.bin		ed1_1000.bin
8000			partitions.bin				ed1_8000.bin
